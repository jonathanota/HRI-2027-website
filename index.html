About the Conference

HRI is the premier international conference for researchers and practitioners advancing Human-Robot Interaction.

Sponsored by ACM SIGCHI, ACM SIGAI, and IEEE RAS, HRI brings together researchers spanning robotics, human-computer interaction, human factors, artificial intelligence, engineering, and social and behavioral sciences. The theme of the 22nd edition — Innovative HRI — explores new frontiers in how humans and robots collaborate, communicate, and coexist.
